Plant-Powered Plates

100% plant-based, 100% delicious

⏱ 5 min Vegan

Mango Avocado Salsa Bowl

A tropical, creamy bowl bursting with fresh flavours — no cooking required.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango, diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh coriander, salt & chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Dice the mango, avocado and tomatoes into a bowl.
  2. Squeeze lime juice over the top and toss gently.
  3. Season with salt, chilli flakes and fresh coriander.
⏱ 8 min Vegan

Spicy Peanut Butter Noodles

Silky noodles tossed in a rich, spicy peanut sauce — comfort food in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • Spring onions & sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cook rice noodles per packet instructions (3-4 min), then drain.
  2. Whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, sriracha and maple syrup with 2 tbsp warm water.
  3. Toss noodles in the sauce. Top with spring onions and sesame seeds.
⏱ 7 min Vegan

Chickpea & Spinach Stir-Fry

A protein-packed skillet dish with warm spices and leafy greens.

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin & smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  2. Add chickpeas, cumin and paprika. Stir-fry 4 minutes until golden.
  3. Toss in spinach and stir until wilted. Season and serve.
⏱ 5 min Vegan

Berry Coconut Smoothie Bowl

A thick, frosty smoothie bowl loaded with antioxidants and crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Toppings: granola, coconut flakes, chia seeds

Steps

  1. Blend frozen berries, banana and coconut milk until thick and smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl.
  3. Top with granola, coconut flakes and chia seeds.
⏱ 6 min Vegan

Smashed Edamame Toast

Creamy, bright green edamame spread on crunchy toast with a citrus kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shelled edamame (defrosted)
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • Chilli flakes, sesame seeds, salt

Steps

  1. Mash edamame with a fork, then stir in tahini, lemon juice and salt.
  2. Toast the sourdough until golden.
  3. Spread edamame mixture on toast. Top with chilli flakes and sesame seeds.
⏱ 8 min Vegan

Black Bean & Corn Quesadilla

Crispy tortillas stuffed with spiced beans, sweet corn and fresh lime.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1 can black beans, drained & mashed
  • 1/2 cup sweetcorn
  • 1 tsp cumin, pinch of chilli
  • Lime wedges, avocado, coriander

Steps

  1. Mix mashed beans with corn, cumin and chilli.
  2. Spread filling on one tortilla, top with the other. Pan-fry 3 min each side.
  3. Slice into wedges. Serve with avocado, lime and coriander.

⏱ 8 min Vegetarian

Caprese Flatbread

Warm, crispy flatbread with melted mozzarella, fresh tomato and basil.

Ingredients

  • 2 naan or flatbreads
  • 1 ball fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze, olive oil, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Place flatbreads under the grill for 2 minutes until lightly crisp.
  2. Layer mozzarella and tomato slices. Grill 3 more minutes until cheese melts.
  3. Top with fresh basil, balsamic glaze and olive oil.
⏱ 7 min Vegetarian

Egg Fried Rice

A classic one-pan dish — fluffy rice, scrambled egg and a punch of soy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil, spring onions

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry rice and peas on high heat for 3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and egg back in. Toss and serve with spring onions.
⏱ 6 min Vegetarian

Greek Yoghurt Veggie Wrap

A cool, crunchy wrap with creamy tzatziki and crisp vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Feta cheese, olives, dried oregano

Steps

  1. Spread Greek yoghurt across each tortilla.
  2. Layer cucumber, tomatoes, crumbled feta and olives.
  3. Sprinkle oregano, roll tightly and slice in half.
⏱ 9 min Vegetarian

Cheesy Garlic Mushrooms on Toast

Golden, buttery mushrooms with garlic and melted cheese on crunchy toast.

Ingredients

  • 250g mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar or gruyere
  • 2 slices sourdough, parsley

Steps

  1. Melt butter. Saute garlic 30 seconds, add mushrooms. Cook 5 minutes.
  2. Toast the sourdough until golden.
  3. Pile mushrooms on toast, top with cheese and grill 1 minute. Garnish with parsley.
⏱ 8 min Vegetarian

Halloumi & Pepper Stir-Fry

Salty, golden halloumi with colourful peppers and a honey-soy glaze.

Ingredients

  • 200g halloumi, sliced into strips
  • 1 red and 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Pan-fry halloumi 2 minutes each side until golden. Set aside.
  2. Stir-fry peppers for 3 minutes on high heat.
  3. Add halloumi back, drizzle honey and soy sauce. Toss and top with sesame seeds.
⏱ 5 min Vegetarian

Ricotta & Honey Toast

Creamy ricotta on warm toast with honey, walnuts and fresh figs.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick bread
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Handful of walnuts, crushed
  • 2 fresh figs, quartered (or berries)

Steps

  1. Toast bread until golden and crispy.
  2. Spread a thick layer of ricotta on each slice.
  3. Top with figs, crushed walnuts and a generous drizzle of honey.

⏱ 8 min Gluten Free

Lemon Herb Salmon

Tender pan-seared salmon with a bright lemon and herb finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon (juice + zest)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh dill or parsley
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Season salmon. Sear skin-side down 4 min, flip and cook 3 more.
  3. Add garlic for the last minute. Squeeze lemon and garnish with herbs.
⏱ 7 min Gluten Free

Sweet Potato & Black Bean Tacos

Smoky, spiced sweet potato and beans in crispy corn tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium sweet potato, grated
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • Corn tortillas (gluten-free)
  • 1 tsp cumin, smoked paprika, chilli
  • Lime, avocado, coriander

Steps

  1. Saute grated sweet potato in olive oil for 4 minutes until soft.
  2. Add black beans and spices. Cook 2 more minutes.
  3. Warm corn tortillas, fill and top with avocado, lime and coriander.
⏱ 5 min Gluten Free

Tuna & White Bean Salad

A Mediterranean-style salad that's fresh, filling and ready in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1 can white beans, drained
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Handful of rocket/arugula
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Combine tuna, white beans, red onion and rocket in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss gently, season and serve.
⏱ 10 min Gluten Free

Thai Coconut Chicken Soup

A fragrant, creamy coconut broth with tender chicken and a kick of chilli.

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp Thai red curry paste (check GF)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • Lime juice, fresh basil or coriander

Steps

  1. Bring coconut milk and curry paste to a simmer, stirring to combine.
  2. Add chicken slices and cook 5-6 minutes until done.
  3. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice. Top with fresh herbs.

Prawn & Avocado Rice Paper Rolls

Light, fresh rolls packed with prawns, avocado and crunchy veg.

Ingredients

  • 6 rice paper wrappers
  • 12 cooked prawns
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • Fresh mint, sweet chilli dipping sauce

Steps

  1. Dip rice paper in warm water 10 seconds until pliable.
  2. Fill with prawns, avocado, carrot and mint.
  3. Roll tightly, tucking in the sides. Serve with sweet chilli sauce.
⏱ 9 min Gluten Free

Steak & Rocket Salad

Juicy seared steak strips on peppery rocket with parmesan shavings.

Ingredients

  • 200g sirloin steak
  • 2 large handfuls of rocket
  • Parmesan shavings
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Season steak. Sear in a hot pan 3 min each side. Rest 2 min.
  2. Slice thinly against the grain.
  3. Arrange rocket, top with steak and parmesan. Drizzle with balsamic.

Iron-Rich Recipes

Boost your energy with iron-packed meals

⏱ 8 min Iron

Seared Liver & Onions

Quick-seared chicken livers with caramelised onions — one of the richest iron sources.

Ingredients

  • 250g chicken livers, trimmed
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt, pepper & splash of balsamic

Steps

  1. Melt butter. Cook onions 3 minutes until softened.
  2. Push onions aside, add livers. Sear 2 min each side.
  3. Add thyme and balsamic. Toss and serve.
⏱ 7 min Iron

Lentil & Spinach Warm Salad

Earthy lentils and wilted spinach with a tangy mustard dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 can lentils, drained
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • Salt, pepper, goat cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Warm lentils in a pan with olive oil for 3 minutes.
  2. Toss in spinach and stir until just wilted.
  3. Whisk mustard and vinegar, drizzle over the top. Season and serve.
⏱ 9 min Iron

Steak Bites with Broccoli

Juicy beef bites with tender broccoli in a garlic-butter sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g sirloin steak, cubed
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Sear steak cubes in a hot pan 3 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Stir-fry broccoli 4 minutes. Add garlic for the last minute.
  3. Return steak, add butter and soy sauce. Toss and serve.

Dark Chocolate & Pumpkin Seed Trail Mix

A no-cook iron-rich snack with dark chocolate, seeds and dried fruit.

Ingredients

Yoghurt Parfait with Almonds

Layers of creamy yoghurt, crunchy almonds and sweet berries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup almonds, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Steps

  1. Layer yoghurt, berries and almonds in a glass or bowl.

⏱ 8 min Calcium

Cheesy Kale & White Bean Saute

Garlicky kale and creamy beans topped with melted parmesan.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups kale, stems removed, chopped
  • 1 can white beans, drained
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil, saute garlic 30 seconds. Add kale, stir until wilted (3 min).
  2. Add white beans and cook 2 more minutes.
  3. Top with parmesan and chilli flakes. Serve warm.

Sardines on Toast with Lemon

Bone-in sardines on crusty toast — a calcium powerhouse.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin sardines in olive oil
  • 2 slices crusty bread
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • Fresh parsley, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Toast bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Arrange sardines on toast, mashing slightly.
  3. Squeeze lemon, scatter capers and parsley. Season and serve.

Tofu & Sesame Stir-Fry

Crispy tofu cubes in a sticky sesame glaze with steamed greens.

Ingredients

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed & patted dry
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • Sesame seeds, steamed pak choi

Steps

  1. Pan-fry tofu in sesame oil on high heat 4 minutes until crispy.
  2. Add soy sauce and honey, toss to glaze 1 minute.
  3. Serve over steamed pak choi, topped with sesame seeds.

Zinc-Packed Recipes

Garlic Butter Prawns

Succulent prawns sizzling in garlic butter with a squeeze of lemon.

Ingredients

  • 300g king prawns, peeled
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley, chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Melt butter. Add garlic and chilli flakes, cook 30 seconds.
  2. Add prawns in a single layer. Cook 2-3 min each side until pink.
  3. Squeeze lemon, scatter parsley and serve.

  • Little gem lettuce leaves
  • Greek yoghurt, fresh mint, pomegranate seeds

Steps

  1. Brown lamb mince 5 minutes, breaking it up as it cooks.

High in Iodine

Iodine-Rich Recipes

Fuel your thyroid with ocean-fresh flavours

⏱ 8 min Iodine

Garlic Butter Cod

Flaky white cod in a simple garlic-lemon butter — quick and elegant.

Ingredients

  • 2 cod fillets
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Fresh parsley, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter over medium heat. Season cod with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook cod 3-4 min each side until flaky. Add garlic in the last minute.
  3. Squeeze lemon, spoon pan butter on top and garnish with parsley.
⏱ 6 min Iodine

Seaweed & Cucumber Salad

A light Japanese-style salad with wakame seaweed and sesame dressing.

Ingredients

  • 10g dried wakame seaweed
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce + 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds, pinch of sugar

Steps

  1. Soak wakame in warm water 5 minutes, then drain and squeeze dry.
  2. Whisk rice vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil and sugar.
  3. Toss wakame and cucumber in the dressing. Top with sesame seeds.
⏱ 7 min Iodine

Prawn & Nori Rice Bowl

Warm rice topped with soy-glazed prawns and crispy nori strips.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 150g cooked prawns
  • 2 nori sheets, cut into strips
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tsp mirin
  • Spring onions, sesame seeds, avocado

Steps

  1. Warm prawns with soy sauce and mirin for 2 minutes.
  2. Serve over warm rice with sliced avocado.
  3. Top with nori strips, spring onions and sesame seeds.
⏱ 10 min Iodine

Creamy Haddock with Spinach

Smoked haddock poached in cream with wilted spinach — rich in iodine.

Ingredients

  • 2 smoked haddock fillets
  • 150ml single cream
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 tbsp butter, pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Place haddock in a pan, pour cream over. Simmer gently 6-7 minutes.
  2. Remove fish. Add butter and spinach to cream, stir until wilted.
  3. Season with nutmeg, salt and pepper. Serve fish on creamy spinach.

High in Omega-3

Omega-3 Rich Recipes

Healthy fats for brain, heart and joints

⏱ 8 min Omega-3

Teriyaki Salmon Rice Bowl

Glazed salmon flaked over warm rice with avocado and pickled ginger.

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 2 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • Pickled ginger, spring onion, sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Pan-sear salmon 3 min each side. Brush teriyaki in the last minute.
  2. Flake salmon over warm rice.
  3. Top with avocado, pickled ginger, spring onion and sesame seeds.
⏱ 5 min Omega-3

Smoked Mackerel Pate on Toast

Rich, smoky mackerel whipped with cream cheese — an omega-3 powerhouse.

Ingredients

  • 2 smoked mackerel fillets, flaked
  • 3 tbsp cream cheese
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tsp horseradish (optional)
  • Toast, fresh dill, black pepper

Steps

  1. Mash mackerel with cream cheese, lemon juice and horseradish.
  2. Season with black pepper.
  3. Spread on toast and garnish with fresh dill.
⏱ 5 min Omega-3

Chia Seed Pudding

A creamy overnight-style pudding that only takes minutes to prep.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup milk (or coconut/almond milk)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Toppings: berries, nuts, coconut flakes

Steps

  1. Stir chia seeds, milk, honey and vanilla in a bowl.
  2. Let sit 5 minutes, stirring once halfway to prevent clumps.
  3. Top with berries, nuts and coconut. (Or refrigerate overnight for thicker set.)
⏱ 6 min Omega-3

Walnut & Flaxseed Banana Smoothie

A thick, nutty smoothie loaded with plant-based omega-3s.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp honey, pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and dust with cinnamon.

Sardine & Avocado Smash

Mashed avocado and sardines on toast — a double dose of healthy fats.

Ingredients

  • 1 tin sardines in olive oil

  • 2 slices bread
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Chilli flakes, salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Toast bread. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Spread avocado on toast.
  3. Top with sardines, a drizzle of their oil, and chilli flakes.
